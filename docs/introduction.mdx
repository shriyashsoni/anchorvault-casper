# Welcome to AnchorVault

<Note>
AnchorVault is currently deployed on **Casper Testnet**. Mainnet deployment coming soon.
</Note>

AnchorVault is a **production-grade, decentralized liquidity protocol** built on the Casper Casper WASM Smart Contract Platform. It bridges **Liquidity Providers (LPs)** with **authorized off-ramp payment anchors** to facilitate instant, cross-border remittances.

LPs lock stablecoin reserves into corridor pools and organic yield is dynamically routed to them from real payment settlement flows.

---

## Why AnchorVault?

Traditional cross-border remittances are plagued by high fees, slow settlement times, and opaque intermediaries. AnchorVault solves this by creating a **trustless, on-chain liquidity layer** that:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Instant Settlements" icon="bolt">
    Payment anchors draw from pre-funded USDC pools to settle remittances in seconds, not days.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Real Yield for LPs" icon="chart-line">
    Liquidity Providers earn organic yield from actual settlement fees — not inflationary token emissions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Trustless & Transparent" icon="lock">
    All operations are governed by Casper WASM smart contracts. No custodians, no opaque middlemen.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dynamic Risk Management" icon="shield">
    Utilization-based fee curves, reputation scoring, and collateral requirements protect LP capital.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Protocol Participants

AnchorVault coordinates three distinct entities trustlessly on-chain:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Liquidity Providers (LPs)">
    Deposit USDC stablecoin into corridor pools. Receive **$AVLT** share tokens representing their pro-rata pool ownership. Earn yield from anchor settlement fees.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Payment Anchors">
    Licensed off-ramp operators who draw USDC from corridor pools to fulfill instant remittance payouts. Must stake collateral and maintain a healthy reputation score.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Smart Contracts">
    Three interconnected Casper WASM contracts (Corridor Vault, Anchor Registry, Vault Token) that orchestrate deposits, withdrawals, draws, repayments, and yield distribution.
  </Step>
</Steps>
