Elevator Pitch
* AnchorVault is a trustless on-chain remittance liquidity routing protocol on Stellar Soroban. It bridges Liquidity Providers with authorized off-ramp payment anchors to facilitate instant, cross-border remittances, replacing slow opaque intermediaries with a transparent smart-contract layer.

Business Model
* We provide a decentralized liquidity layer for cross-border remittances. We sell liquidity access to licensed payment anchors (off-ramps) who draw from our USDC pools to settle payouts instantly. Revenue comes from utilization-based fees charged to anchors on each draw, which are dynamically routed to our Liquidity Providers as organic yield.
